Electric Bike Atlanta

Rents eletric bikes out of co-op working spaces around Atlanta. Great for getting around fast and easy. Eletric bikes are allowed everywhere a regular bike can go but with no effort on your part. Eletric bikes have a 20 mile radius and can reach speeds up to 20 mph. Our bikes come with saddle bags for your valuables aka computer, helmets, and horns.

Interactive Map

The map has two functions. First to let you know where they co-op working spaces are that you rent an electric bike from. Second is to show you a bike route to where you're going and back to the nearest bike rental location
